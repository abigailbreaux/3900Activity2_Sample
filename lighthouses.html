Historic Lighthouses of Door County

Pottawatomie Lighthouse

Pottawatomie Lighthouse, built in 1836, is Wisconsin’s oldest lighthouse and sits on Rock Island.

Eagle Bluff Lighthouse

Located in Peninsula State Park, Eagle Bluff Lighthouse offers tours and breathtaking views of Green Bay waters.

Cana Island Lighthouse

Accessible by a causeway, Cana Island Lighthouse is one of Door County’s most photographed landmarks.
